Model Assumptions

What This Is Built On

These are optimistic but achievable projections. Life will curve differently. The principles don't change.

Physician Specialty Family medicine or general practice. $280–340k attending salary. Own practice opens year 3–4 post-residency with ~$200k startup capital.
Sovereign Notepad Growth Physical product evolves into digital/SaaS offering. Revenue grows 30–40% annually through year 8, then matures at $300–400k/yr or is acquired.
Personal Training Stays intentionally small — private clients, premium pricing. $150–250/hr. 2–4 sessions/week. Never a burden, always a source of energy.
Investment Rate 25% of net income invested from year 1 of attending. Low-cost index funds, real estate considered post-million. 7–8% average annual return assumed.
Education Costs $250k family savings covers most of med school tuition + living. ~$60k small loan taken in year 4. Cleared within 18 months of residency start.
All Figures Nominal Not inflation-adjusted. Real purchasing power will be lower. $24M in 2056 dollars ≈ roughly $10–12M in today's purchasing power. Still life-changing.
